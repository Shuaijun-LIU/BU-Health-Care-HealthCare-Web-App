Welcome to the Boston University Student Health Care Service!

Please select your role to proceed to the respective management interface. We are dedicated to providing a convenient and efficient health service management experience for students, doctors, and administrators.

Student

As a student, you can book appointments, view your health history, and manage your personal information.

Doctor

As a doctor, you can manage student appointments, record diagnoses and prescriptions, and manage patient information.

Administrator

As an administrator, you can manage doctors, students, appointments, and medical records to ensure the smooth operation of the system.
